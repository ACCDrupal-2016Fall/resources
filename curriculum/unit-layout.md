# Unit Layout Format

## Unit Num: Unit Title

* Week Num: Title
  * Unit Num: Unit Title
  * Section Num.1: Section Title
  * Section Num.2: Section Title
  * Section Num.3: Section Title
  * Section Num.*n*: Section Title
  * Unit Num: Summary
  * Unit Num: Further Reading (*Aggregated from all Sections*)
